# S2B2C Business Model - Conceptual Framework

**Layout**: Hierarchical/Centralized
**Style**: Blueprint (Technical schematic, clean lines, professional blueprint aesthetic)

**STRUCTURE**:

- **Central Hub**: labeled "某司 (Company)" - The core orchestrator.
- **Supply Side (Left)**: labeled "品牌方 (Brand Partners)". Arrows pointing to "某司" labeled "联合孵化 / 产品供给".
- **Empowerment Side (Right)**: labeled "经销商 (Distributors: KOL / 微商)". Arrows pointing from "某司" labeled "全链路赋能 (流量/管理/供应链)".
- **Consumer Side (Bottom)**: labeled "消费者 (Consumers)". Arrows pointing from "经销商" labeled "专业服务 / 社交裂变".

**NODES**:

- Company: Large central rectangle with icons of management and technology.
- Brand Partners: Group of circular nodes with factory and product icons.
- Distributors: Cluster of nodes with person/influencer icons.
- Consumers: Broad base of person icons.

**RELATIONSHIPS**:

- Company controls the brand (incubation) and empowers the distributor (fulfillment).
- Distributor is the direct touchpoint for consumers.

**STYLE**: blueprint, technical blueprint, engineering drawing, schematic, architectural style, clean white lines on blue background or professional dark ink on light blueprint paper.
**ASPECT**: 16:9
**LANGUAGE**: Chinese
